How does this game work?

The dispalyed number is the current number, your job is to guess if the next number will be higher or lower then the current number

If you guess correctly the background will turn green and your combo will go up by 1

If you guess incorrectly the background will turn red and your combo will reset to 0 provided you had a combo to begin with
